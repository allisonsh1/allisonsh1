Who are we?

Clover is an organization of high schoolers with a love for math committed to inspiring the same passion among younger students. With our combined expertise, we seek to cultivate a community of mathematicians through organizing and providing engaging opportunities for exploration, learning, and of course, competition.

Locations

We are a Naperville based organization. Clover competitions will most likely be held at Naperville North or Central High School, Benedictine University, Or Kennedy Junior High.
