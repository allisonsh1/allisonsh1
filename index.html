Rise to the challenge!
Welcome to Clover Math! We're a Naperville-based group of experienced high schoolers dedicated to spreading our passion for math among younger students. Whether you're just getting started or you've taken many before, we'd love for you to sign up!
Who should compete
We are open to 8th-grade students and younger of any expertise and background. Whether this is your first competition or your fiftieth, we'd love to have you! Our tests are written with similar difficulties to that of Mathcounts and AMC 10, so would be best suited for middle school students. However, any younger student is just as welcome if they are up for the challenge!
Test format
  • 30 Questions.
  • 60 Minutes.
  • NO Calculator.
  • All questions are free-response.
  • The questions are in increasing difficulty.
  • Questions 1-10 are worth 3 points each, Questions 11-25 are worth 4 points each, and Questions 26-30 are worth 6 points each.
  • There is no penalty for guessing or wrong answers.
About the test
  • Similar difficulty to AMC 8 and 10 and Mathcounts State Sprint.
  • A great opportunity to practice for the AMC and Mathcounts cycles!
  • There will be an optional review session right after the competition ends where the authors will go over all the problems.

Prizes
  • Cash prizes will be awarded to top scorers!
  • $50 for 1st place, $30 for 2nd place, and $20 for 3rd place.
  • The money will be split if there are ties.
  • There will also be team prizes! We will take the top three scorers from each school and sum their scores. The top three high-scoring schools will receive a trophy, so make sure to bring your friends!
  • Prizes will be handed out at the awards ceremony.
FAQ
Pencils, pens, compasses, rulers, and scratch paper are the only allowed materials. Scratch paper will be provided at the contest.
Right after the competition, we will have a review session where the authors discuss all the problems and their solutions. Solutions and awardees will also be released online. Students will also receive an email with solutions attached.
Review sessions will occur right after the competition are completely optional to attend. Contestants can leave at any point or not go at all. There, we will discuss the answers, work out solutions, and answer questions.
The top 3 scorers will be given their cash prizes. Additionally, we will have ribbons and awards for various categories.
We will contact you and figure out a way to get you your award.
