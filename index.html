Rise to the challenge!

Welcome to Clover math! We're a Naperville-based group of experienced high schoolers dedicated to spreading our passion for math among younger students. Whether you're just getting started or you've taken many before, we'd love for you to sign up!

Who should compete

Clover math competitions are open to any lover of math in 8th grade and below. Although our tests often have the same difficulty as MathCounts and AMC 10 competitions so are thus best suited to middle school students, younger students are just as welcome if they're up to the challenge.
